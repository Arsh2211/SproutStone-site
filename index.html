Welcome to SproutStone

Grow your own food anywhere — no tools, no backyard, no experience needed. Our planting bricks are pre-packed with soil, seeds, and nutrients for easy and sustainable gardening. We're on a mission to help people take control of their food supply in a fun and eco-friendly way.

About SproutStone

Food prices in Canada have risen significantly, and environmental concerns are pushing people toward sustainable living. SproutStone addresses these challenges with our easy-to-use, compact planting bricks. Each brick is made from natural materials and includes seeds, nutrients, and instructions — just add water!
